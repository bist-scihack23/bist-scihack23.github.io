Benefits to volunteering at BIST ScienceHack 2023

  • Inspire and engage with young high school students who might be curious, interested, or passionate about science.
  • Attend the closing event and meet various participants from various walks of life (students, young professionals, renowned scientists, private companies, etc.).
  • Get introduced to career opportunities from scientific organizations and companies.
  • Get featured and share your story on the event website and social media.
  • Showcase your leadership in future career opportunities by including your volunteer experience in your CV, resume, and/or LinkedIn.
  • Get a certificate of appreciation from the Barcelona Institute of Science and Technology.

** Please note that certain volunteer roles may have specific requirements or qualifications, and availability may be limited based on the number of qualified applicants. We will do our best to match your skills and interests with an appropriate volunteer role, but we cannot guarantee placement for all applicants.
